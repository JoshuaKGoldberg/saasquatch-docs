How it works

The magic behind Referral SaaSquatch is that we track referrals and automatically give rewards using subscription system integrations with common platforms such as Stripe, Recurly, Braintree, and Zuora. We also give you a simple JavaScript library to include your referral program inside your web app.

squatch.js Client-side

The JavaScript library is how you can show your referral program inside your web app.

What it does

  • Shows the referral program widget popup (it's an iframe)
  • Single sign-on (SSO)
  • Cookie and referral tracking
  • Integrates with your payment form (coupon autofill)

Subscription System Integration Back-end

Back-end payment integration with services like Stripe, Recurly, Braintree and Zuora let us automatically track your customer's lifecycles, and give out credit for successful referrals.

What it does

  • Track subscriptions (and trials periods, and cancellations)
  • Gives credit for successful referrals

Don't see your payment system listed on our website? Tell us about it and we'll get it added.
